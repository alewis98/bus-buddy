What Can Bus Buddy Do For Me

Well, that depends. Bus Buddy works for all bus agencies that use Transloc. See the list of agencies here

If you don't use Transloc, it won't work

If you do use Transloc, Bus Buddy can...

  • Tell you when the next bus is coming
  • Tell you when the bus after that is coming
  • Tell you when the bus after that is coming
  • Tell you what stop the nearest bus is at (because Transloc's time predictions are never wrong)

Usage

"Hey Alexa, ask Bus Buddy when is the next Northline?"
